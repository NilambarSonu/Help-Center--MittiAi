Understanding Your Soil Report

  1. pH Level: 6.0-7.0 is ideal for most crops (neutral soil)
  2. Nitrogen (N): Essential for leaf growth and green color
  3. Phosphorus (P): Supports root development and flowering
  4. Potassium (K): Strengthens plant immunity and disease resistance
  5. Moisture: Check levels before watering to prevent over/under-watering
